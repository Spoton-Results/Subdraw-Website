How It Works

From Setup to Approval in
Four Simple Steps

Most teams are up and running within a single afternoon. No implementation consultant required.

1
Import Your Budgets & Subs
Upload your project budget and subcontractor list. SubDraw maps every line item to the right sub — automatically.
2
Subs Submit Invoices & COs
Subcontractors log in to their portal and submit invoices, change orders, and compliance documents directly in SubDraw.
3
SubDraw Flags Issues Automatically
Our engine checks every submission against the approved budget and contract terms, flagging overruns and missing docs before you review.
4
Approve, Pay & Document
GCs approve clean invoices in one click, trigger payments, collect lien waivers, and store everything in a permanent audit trail.
